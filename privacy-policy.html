WebMas Privacy Policy

Last Updated: v2.1

Secure by Design Fully Transparent Minimal Data

1 Introduction

Welcome to WebMas Color Tools. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Key Principle

We don't collect personal data. Our tools are designed to work without collecting any personally identifiable information. You can use all our color tools without providing any personal information.

2 Data We Collect

Our RGB to HEX converter and other color tools are designed with privacy in mind:

No Personal Information

We do not collect names, emails, or contact details. No registration required.

Anonymous Analytics

Aggregate usage data only. Cannot identify individual users.

Local Storage

Preferences stored on your device only. Not sent to our servers.

HTTPS Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted for security.

  • No Personal Information Collection: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • No Account Required: You can use all our tools without creating an account or providing any personal information.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics through services like Google Analytics to improve our tools. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Local Storage: Some tools may use your browser's local storage to save preferences or recent conversions. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

3 Cookies

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Necessary for website functionality Session
Analytics Understand visitor interactions 2 years
Preference Remember your settings 1 year
Advertising Display relevant ads (keeps tools free) 6 months

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our tools.

4 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand website traffic and usage patterns (anonymous data only).
  • Advertising: Ad networks to display relevant ads (this helps keep our tools free).
  • Fonts: Google Fonts for typography.
  • Icons: Bootstrap Icons for interface icons.

These third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

Third-Party Links

Google Analytics Privacy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Bootstrap Icons: https://icons.getbootstrap.com/#license

Google Fonts: https://fonts.google.com/about

5 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

HTTPS Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted with SSL/TLS

Regular Updates

Security updates and monitoring

Limited Collection

Only collect what's necessary

Secure Hosting

Reliable, secure infrastructure

6 Children's Privacy

Our tools are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

For Parents

We encourage parents to monitor their children's online activities. Our tools are educational and safe for supervised use, but we do not collect any data from children.

7 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Right to access: Request information about data we have
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 9. Since we don't collect personal data, most of these rights may not apply, but we're here to help.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Notification of Changes

We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. For significant changes, we may add a notice on our homepage or send an email to subscribed users.
