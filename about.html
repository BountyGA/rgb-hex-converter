About WebMas Color Tools

Providing free, high-quality color conversion tools for web developers and designers since 2017.

Our Mission

At WebMas, we believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide affordable, reliable, and easy-to-use tools that help developers and designers work more efficiently.

We understand the frustration of searching for simple solutions to common problems. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating tools that "just work" - no signups, no fees, no complications. Every tool we build is designed with user experience as the top priority.

Our Story

WebMas was founded in 2017 by a developer who wanted less limitations. We started with a simple projects mostly locally, and have since expanded to start including full suite of tools.

2017

Foundation

Launched our first RGB to HEX converter with basic functionality. Focused on providing instant, accurate conversions with a clean interface.

2018

Expansion

Added HEX to RGB conversion and improved user interface. Introduced color preview and copy-to-clipboard functionality.

2019

Mobile Optimization

Redesigned all tools for mobile-first experience. Implemented responsive design that works perfectly on all devices.

2020

Tool Suite

Launched additional color tools including color picker and palette generator. Expanded our focus from conversion to comprehensive color tools.

2021-Present

Continuous Improvement

Regular updates, new features, and performance optimizations. Focused on accessibility, speed, and user experience enhancements.

Our Tools

Currently, we offer the following free tools designed to make your workflow more efficient:

RGB to HEX Converter

Convert RGB color values to HEX codes instantly with real-time color preview and one-click copy functionality. Perfect for converting design colors to web formats.

HEX to RGB Converter

Convert HEX color codes to RGB values with mathematical accuracy. Supports 3-digit and 6-digit HEX codes with or without the # prefix.

Color Picker (Coming Soon)

Interactive color picker with multiple color format support. Visual color selection with real-time conversion between formats.

Color Palette Generator (Coming Soon)

Generate beautiful color palettes for your design projects. Create harmonious color schemes based on color theory principles.

Color Contrast Checker (Coming Soon)

Check WCAG compliance for color contrast ratios. Ensure your designs are accessible to all users.
