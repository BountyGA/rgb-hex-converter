About WebMas Color Tools

Providing free, high-quality color conversion tools for web developers and designers since 2017.

Our Mission

At WebMas, we believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide free, reliable, and easy-to-use color conversion tools that help developers and designers work more efficiently.

We understand the frustration of searching for simple solutions to common problems. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating tools that "just work" - no signups, no fees, no complications.

Our Story

WebMas was founded in 2017 by developers who were tired of the limitations of existing color conversion tools. We started with a simple RGB to HEX converter and have since expanded to include a full suite of color tools.
